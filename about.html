About Me

I'm just a 24 year old regular Joe from Sweden, ordinary by day, digital marketing superhero by night. No funny business. My name is Ioan Petras, but friends call me Jan. Some people are analytical, some are creative. I consider myself to be both and none in the same time. I'm just as interested and experienced in both mathematics, statistics and engineering (Computer Science mostly) as I am with marketing, advertising and business. On the other side, I'm creative, I enjoy writing, reading, painting and anything design related. I tried combining all these things and taking care of each and every one of them all throughout my education and work experience and I think I managed to combine them fully.
I take pleasure from getting to do what I enjoy most, and because a lot of things fall into that category, I would say I'm a pretty happy person all-around.

My Mission

I live in Stockholm, Sweden and work as a Digital Marketing Assistant at AIESEC Sweden. Apart from that and constant work on www.ioanpetras.com, I was put on this Earth with one reason and one reason only. To live my life. I enjoy doing what I do and I consider this living my life. I'm happy with it and I took it as a personal mission to rid the world of wasted time and money with bad web development, digital marketing and business development practices. Whenever and in any way you might need saving, I will be there to provide it and complete another step of my mission.
